Pyramids of giza

The pyramids of Giza consist of the Great Pyramid, the pyramid of Khufu, and there are several meters away from it the Pyramid of Khafre, which is slightly smaller than the pyramid of Khufu. The third pyramid is the Pyramid of Menkaure. There is also a Sphinx in the area as well. 1- Pyramid of Khufu The pyramid of Khufu is the largest pyramid, and the construction date of the pyramid of Khufu is 2551 BC. It is one of the most important achievements of Pharaonic engineering. And it took twenty years to build it. It is 147 meters high and 2300 stone blocks were used in its construction, the average mass of which weighs approximately 2.5 tons. 2- Pyramid of Khafre The Pyramid of Khafre is hundreds of meters away from the pyramid of Khufu. It is slightly lower in height than the pyramid of Khufu. Its construction date is 2520 BC. King Khafre, the son of King Khufu, ordered that this pyramid be built on a site near the pyramid of his father. 3- The Pyramid of Menkaure The king built the square of the third pyramid in the Giza cemetery. Near the pyramid of his father Khafre. The length of this pyramid is approximately 70 meters. Its construction date is 2490 BC. The red granite , which Aswan is famous for, was used to build the base of this pyramid, while its top was built of polished white limestone.

The Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most famous tourist attractions in the world. It is a steel tower with a height of three hundred and forty-two meters. The Eiffel Tower was erected on the occasion of the international exhibition held in Paris in 1889. It was founded by Gustav Eiffel and his assistant. It has been named as the 300 meter tower, and the Eiffel Tower has become the symbol of the French capital of Paris. It is the most visited site in France and enjoys a very impressive beauty and enjoys a very high reputation among all the world’s tourist sites. Although there are many tourist sites in Paris, but the Eiffel Tower, the most important. The Eiffel Tower is located in most Paris regions due to its high altitude. The Eiffel Tower is located in the European continent, specifically in France, in the capital Paris, in the north-west of Champ de Mars. The tower was officially inaugurated on the 31st of March 1889, and its facade was initially exposed to corrosion. The tower did not achieve real success until after 1960. The tower is open all year round for tourist attraction and receives millions of visitors from All over the world. The tower was built in about a year and a half with the participation of 50 engineers. The design and construction work was carried out by the architect Stephen Sophistry, with about 300 workers. It was added to the UNESCO World Heritage in 1991. It consists of 2.5 million nails and weighs about 10 tons , And the tower consists of 18,038 pieces of iron, and the height of three floors above the ground. There are seven elevators, and it is painted with three different degrees of paint color, darker in the base, and the lighter colors in the center and above. It is worth mentioning that the tower contains several restaurants and cafés, Many of the world’s most famous chefs serve in these restaurants..

Tokyo the capital of Japan

Tokyo was once just a small fishing town, but from historical developments it has become one of the most famous cities in the world. Tokyo is not only the capital of Japan, but it is one of the most advanced countries in the world in several fields, the most famous of which is technology. As for the population of Tokyo, their number is about 30% of the population of Japan. The climate in Tokyo is similar to that of the tropics, with hot and humid summers and cold and humid winters. Strong typhoons occur in the fall, when they cause some losses.Tokyo has an estimated population of 37,435,191 people, with about 125 million Japanese speaking people. As for the religion in Japan, it is Buddhism and Shintoism, and it has a famous shrine called Meiji, which people go to a lot.

Burj Khalifa in Dubai

Burj Khalifa is a skyscraper located in the United Arab Emirates, specifically in the Emirate of Dubai. The tower is one of the highest buildings built by human hands. It is the tallest tower in the world with a height of about 828 meters. Burj Khalifa was built on the 21st of September 2004, and the construction of the external structure continued until October 1, 2009. The tower was opened on the fourth of January of 2010, in the presence of Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum. The total area of ​​Burj Khalifa Tower is about 4 million square meters. The tower has 180 floors, a 403-room hotel, 57 electric lifts, where the total cost of construction of this project amounted to about one and a half billion US dollars. A special ceremony was held for the opening of Burj Khalifa, which was attended by many dignitaries such as Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President of the United Arab Emirates and Ruler of Dubai, Where he has changed the name of the tower from the Burj of Dubai to Burj Khalifa; in recognition of the great efforts made by Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, President of the UAE, and the great support he extended to the Emirate of Dubai and other Emirates. Burj Khalifa is home to many parties organized on New Year’s Eve each year, in which there are many events, including the launch of large quantities of fireworks with a wonderful and beautiful view, in addition to dancing Dubai Fountain, which gives the place a beautiful and distinctive view. All these and other things have made this tower a unique tourist destination, visited by huge numbers of tourists from around the world every year.
